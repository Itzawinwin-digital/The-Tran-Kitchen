BEFORE YOU ORDER

Planning made simple.

These answers cover the most common questions so you can decide faster and order with confidence.

Lead time Custom cakes are best booked early.
Design help You can send inspiration photos and color ideas.
Pickup details Pickup and delivery options can be discussed on the form.
How far in advance should I order?

A week or more is ideal for most cakes, and weddings or larger custom orders should be booked earlier whenever possible.

Do you make custom flavors and fillings?

Yes. You can request flavors, fillings, and finish details that fit your event and taste.

How many servings do your cakes feed?

Serving counts depend on cake size, layers, and design. Share your guest count in the form and I'll help you choose the right size.

Do you offer pickup or delivery?

Pickup is available, and delivery can be arranged for select orders. We can work out the details together after you send an inquiry.

Can I send inspiration photos?

Absolutely. Photos, sketches, and color references are welcome and help shape the final design.

How does payment work?

A deposit may be required to reserve your date, with the remaining balance handled before pickup or delivery.
